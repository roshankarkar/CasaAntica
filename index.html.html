Our Story

A Legacy of Timeless Design

Casa Antica was born from a singular vision — to bring world-class furniture craftsmanship to India. We curate only the finest pieces that blend Italian minimalism, artisan woodworking, and contemporary aesthetics into spaces of remarkable beauty.

Our showroom is more than a store — it is an experience. Walk through carefully arranged living tableaux that inspire and ignite the imagination of what your home or office could become.

Premium Materials
Solid hardwoods, genuine leathers, and imported fabrics from the world's finest suppliers.
Master Craftsmen
Every joint, finish and detail executed by artisans with decades of experience.
Bespoke Service
Custom sizing, finishes and configurations tailored precisely to your vision.
White Glove Delivery
Professional installation and placement by our trained delivery specialists.
